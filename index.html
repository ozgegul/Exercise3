Chocolate Mousse Recipe

For 4 People - Preparation Time:15 min

Ingredients:

  • 1 Packet of Cream (200ml)
  • 170gr Bitter Chocolate
  • 1 Egg Yolk
  • 100ml Milk

Directions:

  1. Get half packet of cream and 100ml milk in a cup. Heat for a few minutes.
  2. Get another cup. Put egg yolk in it then start adding the cream and milk slowly while mixing it.
  3. While the mix is warm, add the chocolate. Continue mixing till it melts. Then let it get cold.
  4. Get another cup and put rest of the cream in it. Mix it till it gets tick.
  5. Put the tick cream that you prepared into the chocolate mixture and slowly stir it with a spatula.
  6. Put the new creamy mixture into cups and let it cold for couple of hours.
  7. Enjoy it!
